Aim

The goal of the bachelor thesis is to study the task of body measurements estimation,
test selected state-of-the-art methods, and evaluate them using available benchmark data.



Annotation

Human body measurements estimation is a task that attracts the attention of several scientific fields in recent years. An automatic and accurate approach to this problem is crucial in various areas of the computer vision-oriented industry. Garment manufacturing and tailoring are some of the applications, where an accurate body measurements estimation from visual human data would be beneficial, replacing the traditional manual tape measuring.
